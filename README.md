# website2 — Peacock Gap Shot Plan (Red Tees)

A personal, phone-friendly shot plan for **Peacock Gap Golf Club** (San Rafael, CA) from the **red tees**.
Open `index.html` in any browser. Everything runs locally — no internet needed once loaded, so it works on the course.

## What it does
- All 18 holes: par, stroke index, and your red-tee yardage (from your own workbook).
- A **smart plan per hole** — which club off the tee, where to lay up, and the approach club — tuned to *your* distances.
- **Editable bag:** tap **My Bag** to set your real carry yardages, **add** any club you carry, or **remove** ones you don't. The tee-shot club is always your longest club, so removing the driver just promotes the next one. Every hole and the full card update instantly.
- **Per-hole hazard editor:** add the bunkers and water you know, positioned by **carry distance from the tee** (or green-side). The diagram plots them accurately with carry labels, plus 150/100-yard reference lines.
- **Full card** tab: a one-screen overview / printable summary of the whole round.
- Verified course facts baked in: water fronts the par-3 **6th** and an inlet fronts the par-3 **16th** ("never short") — pre-loaded as hazards.

> Hole diagrams are **schematic** — drawn to your distances to show shot shape, targets, and the hazards *you* place, not satellite-accurate maps. Your bag and hazards are saved on your device.

## Put it online for free (no coding, ~2 minutes)
1. Go to **github.com**, sign in, click **New repository**, name it **`website2`**, make it **Public**, click **Create**.
2. On the new repo page, click **uploading an existing file**. Drag in **`index.html`** (and this README). Click **Commit changes**.
3. Open the repo's **Settings → Pages**. Under "Branch", pick **main** / **/(root)**, click **Save**.
4. Wait ~1 minute, refresh that page, and your live link appears (like `https://YOURNAME.github.io/website2/`).

That link works on your phone — add it to your home screen for one-tap access at the course.
